# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-01

A Boy Raps ("we", "our", "us") provides a mobile app for storing, organizing, converting, and collaborating on audio files.

## Information we collect
1. Account information: username, email, authentication identifiers.
2. User content: audio/video files you upload and metadata you provide.
3. Usage information: app events needed for reliability, billing, and security.
4. Device and diagnostic data: crash logs and performance metrics.

## How we use information
1. Provide core app functionality.
2. Authenticate users and secure accounts.
3. Enforce subscription entitlements and storage limits.
4. Detect abuse, fraud, and security issues.
5. Improve app quality and reliability.

## Sharing
1. Collaborators can access files/albums you explicitly share.
2. We use infrastructure providers (for example AWS) to process and store data.
3. We do not sell personal data.

## Retention
1. Account and content data are retained while your account is active.
2. You may request deletion of your account and associated content.
3. Some logs may be retained for legal/security reasons.

## Your choices
1. Update your profile information in-app.
2. Reset your password in-app.
3. Request account deletion through support.

## Children
A Boy Raps is not directed to children under 13.
